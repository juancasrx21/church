Welcome to St. Mary's Catholic Church

Our Parish

St. Mary's Catholic Church has been serving our community for nearly a century. Our parish is dedicated to spreading the Gospel of Jesus Christ and serving all people with compassion and love.

We welcome all who seek to deepen their faith and relationship with God through prayer, worship, and community service.
